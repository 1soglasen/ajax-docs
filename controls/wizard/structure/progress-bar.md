# Progress Bar



__ProgressBar__ shows the current level of completion of the wizard steps.

## 
>caption Figure 1: RadWizard Progress bar

![wizard-progress-bar](images/wizard-progress-bar.png)

The ProgressBar position is controlled by the __ProgressBarPosition__ property. This property value accepts one of the following values: __Top__, __Bottom__, __Left__ or __Right__.

>caution NavigationBar and ProgressBar can be only placed either on the same side or on opposite sides of the RadWizard control.
>
